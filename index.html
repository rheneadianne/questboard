Bandits at Butterskull Ranch

Redbrands have attacked Butterskull Ranch!! Make haste!! The ranch is 5 miles east of Triboar Trail. Assess the damage and help in any way that you can. The ranch owner Alfonso "Big Al" Kalazorn is a retired sheriff who can reward you for your efforts. If Big Al is dead, please return to Town Hall with proof and show Town Advisor Dmitri Aelsinorinh.

Do NOT show dead bodies to Town Master Harbin Wester. You and potential companions will be rewarded 25 GP each should the sheriff be dead and you return with proof of his demise.

Escort to Mountain's Toe

The new over-seer Don-Jon Jarnskin needs to be escorted to Mountain's Toe Gold Mine. It is IMPERATIVE that he make it to the gold man. Bandits! Beasts!! Dragon(s??)!!!. Don-Jon offered 25 GP to you/your party in advance. Come back to Harbin Wester with a letter confirming Don-Jon Jarnskin's safety for another 25 GP.

Scope out Axeholm

An ancient dwarven fortress named Axeholm has been sealed for ages. In the event of calamity the people of Phandalin could use the space for refuge.
Open the fortress, ensure that it's habitable, and return to Town Advisor Dmitri.

Reward: 250 GP.

Rumours of a Dragon Slaying Sword

Phandalin was part of an empire of a Tyrannical elven king rumoured to be a dragon slayer. His sword is buried in the barrow where the dragon was once slain.
FInd the sword and it's yours.

Reward: The sword is it's own reward.

Warn Dwarves at Excavation of TERRIFYING Dragon

Dwarf prospectors found ancient dwarven ruins in the mountains southwest of here. They MUST be warned that the DREADED white dragon has moved (and is STILL MOVING!!) somewhere in the area. Please!! Take this warning to them and return to me (Townmaster Harbin Wester), OR my advisor Dmitri Aelsinorinh for a reward of 50 GP.

Deliver Supplies to Logger's Camp

There lies a logger's camp deep in the Neverwinter Woods, slightly west of Falcon's Hunting Lodge ran by Tibor Wester. Pick up supply bags from Linene Graywind at Lionshield. You'd probably need to camp out at Falcon's for a little bit. I'll give you some funds for a 3 nights stay. If you use it for something else good luck sleeping at his guest house. You can smell the horse manure if you open the windows even the slightest...

Reward: 10 GP each for your troubles. + 25% coupon at Lionshield for your full purchase.

Get magical inventions from clever gnomes at Gnomengarde to beat MONSTROUS dragon

Deep in the mountains of the southeast is a small clan of clever rock gnomes (though they're quite the recluses). They call themselves the gnomes of Gnomengarde. Nonetheless, they're crafty and have a knack for magical weapons. Find them and buy some magical items that would be useful in felling the horrid beast that roams our once peaceful skies. Please have the gnomes print a receipt.

You may keep all magical items ONLY IF!!! You SWEAR to aide the plight in killing the Great White Menace.
